Grandway Accounting Services

Established in 2010, we are a team of qualified professionals specialising in the field of accounting, audit and tax; we also provide advisory and consulting services to our clients when required. Individually, our team members are experienced in the audit of entities ranging from trusts and funds to entities listed on the JSE Stock Exchange. As a firm, we are experienced in accounting and audit services for small and medium sized businesses as well as providing set-up and expansion solutions to start-up firms. Our Small Business Services (SBS) packages have been a success in helping start-ups and small business grow and expand through innovative internal control strategies and reporting channel setups. Our Back-Office Administration (BOA) packages aids medium to large companies save overheads in specific accounting functions through our outsourcing services.

We help our clients interpret and apply the accounting standards from record keeping to presentation and disclosure on the annual financial statements.

Audit

An audit of the annual financial statements is beneficial to an entity not only from the attest function provided by the auditors but also the value adding product of the auditor's risk based approach carried out during the audit. Note that the Companies Act of 2008 allows privately owned companies the option of not having the annual financial statements audited; this takes the burden off small owner managed companies in respect of the audit fees.

Bookkeeping

We provide bookkeeping services to our clients if requested; bookkeeping is the recording of financial transactions to the trial balance level.

Company Secretarial Services

The Companies Act sets out administrative rules that requires every company to document meetings, decisions and directors interest in the dealing of the company. Where a business employs staff, they need to register with the Unemployment Insurance Fund (UIF) and the Compensation Fund. We advise our clients on the legislative requirements for a company and help maintain and submit documentation to relevant authorities.

Tax

With the tax laws constantly changing, it is hard for business owners to keep up-to-date with the latest changes. We work with our clients and keep them updated on the developments in the rules relevant to them. We also assist businesses in the following:
  • Monthly tax returns (PAYE, UIF, SDL)
  • Bi-monthly VAT returns
  • Bi-annual employer's reconciliation for employees tax
  • Provisional (IRP6) and yearend (IT14) tax returns
  • Tax advisory

Audit File Preparation

An audit is an expensive exercise for any business; audit fees are based on the time a team of professionals take to gather enough information to be able to satisfy themselves that the annual financial statements of an entity is not materially misstated. We help an entity gather and arrange documents required for an audit so the audit can be performed efficiently thus creating a saving in the audit fees.
